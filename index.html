About Us!

Welcome to LEIRA HOLDING, a dynamic and forward-thinking company that specializes in building mega projects with a focus on the future. As a relatively new player in the industry, LEIRA HOLDING pursues strategic, sustainable, and economically feasible investments in vital and promising sectors, as well as viable long-term projects, with the goal of creating a clear and sustainable socioeconomic impact and aligning its objectives with the global strategic investment direction.

Agriculture

LEIRA HOLDING leverages cutting-edge technology as well as agricultural know-how to develop plants in an environment fully under our control. We are the first to introduce the concept of the highest vertical farm tower using the latest technology and scientific knowledge. We deploy sophisticated technologies that have been customized for our vertical farm. We provide the most immaculate and hygienic items, all of which are pesticide-free, have a long shelf life and derive their flavor from the same source. Mother Nature's pattern is something we stick to. In addition, we use state-of-the-art sensors and data analytics to monitor and optimize every phase of the growing process, ensuring that our crops are healthy and nutritious. By building the highest vertical farm tower, we are trying to maximize the volume of the produce we can grow in a small space, making it an efficient and sustainable solution for feeding a growing population. Our innovative approach is set to revolutionize the way we think about agriculture and its production.
